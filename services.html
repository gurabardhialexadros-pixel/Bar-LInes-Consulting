Direction

About the step

We start by understanding your venue — not just what you want, but what is actually happening behind the bar.

From menu structure and service flow to team performance and cost control, we identify where things break down and where the real opportunity lies. This is where clarity replaces guesswork.

What this includes

  • Full menu & beverage review — cocktails, wine, and overall structure
  • Bar workflow and service flow assessment
  • Costing, margins & profitability check
  • Team structure and performance evaluation
  • Identification of operational gaps and inefficiencies

Why it matters

Most venues don't have a clear problem — they have hidden ones. This step defines exactly what needs to change before anything is built.

About the step

Once direction is clear, we design the solution.

From cocktail menus and wine integration to SOPs, prep systems, and pricing models — everything is built to work in real conditions, not just look good on paper. Every detail is engineered for consistency, speed, and profitability.

What this includes

  • Cocktail menu development & wine pairing strategy
  • Recipe specs, batching & prep systems
  • Costing models and pricing structure
  • SOP creation and operational frameworks
  • Staff workflow design and service structure

Why it matters

Ideas are easy. Execution isn't. This step ensures everything is built to perform under pressure — not just during planning.
